What came first? Chicken or egg?

The question of which came first, the chicken or the egg, is a classic philosophical conundrum with no definitive answer. According to evolutionary biology, chickens evolved from non-chicken ancestors over millions of years through a gradual process of genetic mutations and natural selection. Therefore, it’s possible that an egg laid by a non-chicken ancestor of the chicken species mutated to produce the first true chicken. Alternatively, it’s also possible that the first true chicken was born from a non-chicken egg that had undergone a genetic mutation. So, the answer to this question is not straightforward and still debated among scientists and philosophers.
